How we choose our picks

We don’t rely on flashy specs or lab numbers alone. Instead, we compile and cross-reference feedback from long-term owners on forums, subreddits, and retail review sections, looking for patterns that reveal which lights actually survive drops, water exposure, and daily pocket carry. We also study published reviews from respected independent testers (like 1Lumen and ZeroAir) to understand beam profiles, driver efficiency, and build quality. What breaks matters as much as what shines. We pay close attention to failure points: a switch that starts flickering, a lens that cracks, a battery tube that won’t hold a spring. Our picks are lights with proven track records for durability and customer support, not just first-impression dazzle.
